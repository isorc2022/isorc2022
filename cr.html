CAMERA READY SUBMISSION

Camera Ready Version

This page contains information required for authors with accepted papers in order to prepare the camera ready version.

Please complete the three steps outlined here and described in detail below before the camera ready deadline on April 20.

  1. Add the appropriate copyright notice to your paper.
  2. Submit the final version to PDFExpress. Note that the EasyChair website is for review only and the actual camera-ready version must be submitted via PDFExpress.
  3. The first corresponding author will receive an email from IEEE with instructions to transfer the copyright to IEEE. Note that one author can sign the form on behalf of all co-authors.

Adding IEEE Copyright Notice to your Paper

The appropriate copyright clearance code notice is to appear at the bottom of the first page of each paper according to the guidelines set forth in the Cataloging/Copyright Instructions for an IEEE Conference Proceeding. The copyright notice should appear about 1 cm below the left column, and should be aligned to the left with the same left margin as the column.

The copyright notice to be added depends on the circumstances as follows:

For papers in which all authors are employed by the US government, the copyright notice is:

U.S. Government work not protected by U.S. copyright

For papers in which all authors are employed by a Crown government (UK, Canada, and Australia), the copyright notice is:

978-1-6654-0627-7/22/$31.00 ©2022 Crown

For papers in which all authors are employed by the European Union, the copyright notice is

978-1-6654-0627-7/22/$31.00 ©2022 European Union

For all other papers the copyright notice is:

978-1-6654-0627-7/22/$31.00 ©2022 IEEE

Tips for adding the IEEE copyright notice to your pdf:

You may copy and paste the piece of code below into your source code file in the preamble, i.e, between the "\documentclass" and "\begin{document}" commands in your TeX file. Please substitute the copyright string for the one suitable for your publication.

\IEEEoverridecommandlockouts
\IEEEpubid{\makebox[\columnwidth]{978-1-6654-0627-7/22/\$31.00~\copyright2022 IEEE\hfill}
\hspace{\columnsep}\makebox[\columnwidth]{ }}

PDF Express Compatibility of Camera-Ready Version

  • Add the copyright notice to the bottom of the first page of your source document. If necessary, contact Matthias Becker at mabecker@kth.se for the appropriate copyright notice (see “Adding IEEE Copyright Notice to your Paper” above).
  • Proofread your source document thoroughly to confirm that it will require no revision.

Creating your PDF eXpress Account

Log in to the IEEE PDF eXpress TM site (https://ieee-pdf-express.org/ )

First-time users should do the following:

  1. Select the New Users - Click Here link(https://ieee-pdf-express.org/account/signup ).
  2. Enter the following:
    • 52572X for the Conference ID
    • your email address
    • a password
  3. Continue to enter information as prompted.
  4. Approve the pdf for collection.

An Online confirmation will be displayed and an email confirmation will be sent verifying your account setup.

Previous users of PDF eXpress need to follow the above steps, but should enter the same password that was used for previous conferences. Verify that your contact information is valid.

Contacting PDF eXpress Support

Access the Publications Support Center(https://supportcenter.ieee.org/app/answers/list/p/12 ) for IEEE PDF eXpress.

If you do not find an answer in the Find Answers tab, go to the “Ask A Question” tab.

Your question will be forwarded to IEEE PDF Support and answered within 3 business days.

Transfer of Copyright

We use the IEEE Electronic Copyright Form to transfer the copyright for the publications.

For this, the first corresponding author will receive an email from IEEE. This email will provide the authors with a link to the online eCF wizard, as well as a unique login name and password to access their own copyright forms. When an author completes the online copyright transfer process and submits the form, he/she will receive an automated confirmation email letting him/her know that the transfer has been completed successfully.
